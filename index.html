The idea

One question becomes a multi-agent debate.

People do not just need one fast answer. For important questions, they need a multi-level response: different expert agents with specific perspectives looking at the same question.

One agent may focus on risk. Another on daily habits. Another on emotional context. Another on practical next steps. When those perspectives debate each other, the answer becomes more robust, more entertaining, and easier to trust than a single chatbot reply.

Rollout timeline

Earn the right to keep going.

May 19–Jun 5

Build the Health proof

Show a health question becoming a Roundtable of expert agents, then a useful recap.

Assets needed: Health demo flow, thirty-second trailer draft, questions-and-answers page, TVBS / Health 2.0 partner one-pager, terms + health disclaimer.

1
Decision · Jun 5–6

Is the first experience working?

Users finish a first useful Roundtable. Sharing measurement is ready: share clicks, shared-link opens, and new sessions from shared links are recorded.

Assets needed before this gate: measurement checklist and working share tracking.

Jun 6–Jun 30

Open beta: learn in public, but do not call it fully launched

Health stays the main story. Food, tech, sport, and finance appear as secondary examples and tests.

Assets needed: six-second hook cut, landing-page embed, four secondary topic demos, shareable recap preview.

Jul 1–Jul 31

Watch whether people share it and return

A shared link should open the same topic and expert setup so the next person can start their own session.

Assets needed: working shareable link design, preview card, simple metrics dashboard.

2
Decision · Jul 31–Aug 2

Is this product pulling people back?

Only move bigger if people share it, receivers start sessions, users spend time in the debate, and people return without being pushed.

Assets needed before this gate: metrics dashboard, share-link data, and beta user feedback summary.

Aug 3–Aug 28

Full public launch and second topic choice

Food, tech, sport, and finance all get small tests. Choose the next topic by share rate, time in session, and repeat use — not opinion.

Assets needed: Product Hunt page if Gate 2 passes, press note, second-topic landing section, founder demo script.

3
Decision · Aug 28

Can this become a paid partner test?

A partner such as TVBS tries Roundtable inside their app with real users for thirty or sixty days. Both sides agree what success means before it starts.

Assets needed before this gate: partner test brief, partner rollout checklist, support owner, analytics owner.

Aug 31–Oct 23

Run paid partner tests and lock the business model

Only work with partner leads or user accounts that already show real usage.

Assets needed: partner proposal template, pricing notes, customer support plan, legal review path.

4
Decision · Oct 23

Is it ready to scale?

Only expand topics, partners, or markets when repeat use, sharing, and partner proof are real.

Assets needed before this gate: partner results summary, rollout case study, Asia-Pacific expansion checklist.
